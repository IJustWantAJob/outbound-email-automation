# Example Campaign -- Sample Outreach

This is a template campaign showing the file structure and format expected by the parser and sending app. All company names, contacts, and data below are entirely fictional.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- **Target:** Commercial service companies that maintain equipment or systems
- **Industry:** (Replace with your industry)
- **Company size:** 10-200 employees, $2M-$50M revenue
- **Decision maker:** Owner, VP of Operations, Service Manager, Director
- **Geography:** (Replace with your target region)
- **Buying signal:** Offers service contracts, advertises monitoring/maintenance, mentions technology adoption

## Volume Targets
- Target: 30 contacts
- Waves: 5 (highest response likelihood first)

## File Map

| File | What It Is |
|------|-----------|
| `example_outreach_campaign.md` | **START HERE.** Master campaign file with all emails + follow-ups in parser-compatible format. |
| `README.md` | This file. Campaign overview, ICP, file map. |
| `target_companies.md` | Target companies with descriptions, locations, and why they were selected. |
| `contacts.md` | Decision-maker contacts with email addresses, confidence levels, LinkedIn URLs. |
| `emails.md` | Personalized initial outreach emails with subject lines and response likelihood scores. |
| `followups.md` | Follow-up emails (2 per contact) with timing guide. |

### Reading Order

1. **To run the campaign:** Open `example_outreach_campaign.md` -- it has everything you need.
2. **To understand company selection:** Read `target_companies.md` for the research behind each company.
3. **To verify contacts:** Cross-reference `contacts.md` for LinkedIn URLs, phone numbers, and alt contacts.
4. **To see email strategy:** `emails.md` has wave response expectations. `followups.md` has follow-up timing.

## Email Tone

Customize this to match your profile's tone. Default: curious, peer-to-peer. You are genuinely curious about their problems. NOT a sales pitch. Ask for 15 minutes to learn about their challenges.
