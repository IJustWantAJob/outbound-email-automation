## HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

This is your complete, ready-to-send outreach campaign. Everything is in one place.

1. **Start with Section 1 (Sending Calendar)** -- it tells you exactly who to email on what day, in what order.
2. **Copy-paste emails from Section 2** -- each contact has the initial email + 2 follow-ups, ready to send.
3. **When someone replies, stop all follow-ups** for that contact immediately. Move to conversation mode -- ask discovery questions, don't pitch.
4. **Max 2 follow-ups per contact.** After Follow-Up #2, move to LinkedIn or phone if no response.
